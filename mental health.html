Common Mental Health Illnesses

Bipolar Disorder

Symptoms include extreme mood swings, ranging from manic episodes of elevated mood and energy to depressive episodes of low mood and energy.

Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)

Symptoms include recurring, unwanted thoughts (obsessions) and repetitive behaviors (compulsions).

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)

Symptoms include flashbacks, nightmares, severe anxiety, and uncontrollable thoughts about a traumatic event.

Depression

Symptoms include persistent sadness, loss of interest in activities, changes in appetite, and feelings of hopelessness.
