Coping Strategies

Here are some tips to help you manage stress and improve your mental well-being:

  • Practice mindfulness and meditation.
  • Engage in regular physical activity.
  • Maintain a healthy diet.
  • Get enough sleep.
  • Connect with loved ones and seek support when needed.

Helpful Resources

Here are some resources that can provide additional support and information:

  • MentalHealth.gov - A comprehensive resource for mental health information and support.
  • NAMI - The National Alliance on Mental Illness provides education, support, and advocacy for individuals affected by mental illness.
  • American Psychiatric Association - Offers resources and information on mental health conditions and treatments.
  • SAMHSA - The Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration provides resources for mental health and substance use disorders.
  • WHO Mental Health Resources that help regulate people living with mental health illnesses
